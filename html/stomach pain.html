Abdominal & stomach pain causes & treatment:
We all have stomachaches, upset stomach, and abdominal pain from time to
time, and they’re usually not serious. But they can be a sign that something
inside your body is not functioning the way it should – especially if you have
severe stomach pain or sharp stomach pain. Causes can be anything from gas or
a pulled muscle to something as serious as an internal infection or heart attack.
